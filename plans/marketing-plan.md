## Lead Generation Tactics

### Free Strategies
1. **Cold outreach via Facebook/Instagram DMs**
   - Find local businesses with no website or bad website
   - Send: "Hi! I noticed [business] doesn't have a website yet. I help small businesses in [town] get online. Would you be interested in a free chat about it?"

2. **Google Maps audit**
   - Search businesses in your area on Google Maps
   - If they have no website listed, offer your services

3. **Facebook Groups**
   - Be helpful first, sell later
   - When someone asks for a web designer, you're already known

4. **Referral programme**
   - Give existing clients £50 for each referral
   - Give the new client 10% off

5. **Free website audit**
   - Offer to review someone's existing website for free
   - Send them a PDF with improvements → offer to fix them

### Paid Strategies
1. **Facebook/Instagram Ads** — from £5/day
2. **Google Ads** — target "web designer near me", "affordable website UK"
3. **Sponsored local events** — get your name out there
4. **Printed materials** — business cards, flyers in local shops
