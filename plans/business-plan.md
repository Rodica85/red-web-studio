# RED — Business Plan

## 1. Business Overview

**Name:** RED
**Tagline:** "We Build. You Grow."
**Location:** United Kingdom
**Industry:** Web Design & Digital Services for Small Businesses
**Founded:** 2026

### Mission
To help small local businesses in the UK establish a strong online presence through affordable, professional websites that drive real results.

### Vision
Become the go-to web design partner for small businesses across the UK — known for quality, speed, and results.

## 3. Target Market

### Primary Audience
- Small local businesses in the UK
- Restaurants, cafés, pubs
- Hair salons, barbers, beauty clinics
- Tradespeople (plumbers, electricians, builders)
- Fitness studios, personal trainers
- Local shops and boutiques
- Professional services (accountants, solicitors, dentists)

### Customer Profile
- Business owner, 25-55 years old
- Limited tech knowledge
- Budget-conscious but wants professional results
- Needs a website but doesn't know where to start
- Values personal service and clear communication

## 9. Growth Plan

### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1-3)
- Launch website & social media
- Build 3-5 portfolio pieces (offer discounted rates)
- Set up Google Business Profile
- Start creating TikTok/Instagram content

### Phase 2: Growth (Month 4-6)
- Start Facebook Ads (£5-10/day)
- Collect testimonials and case studies
- Join local business groups
- Launch referral programme

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 7-12)
- Increase ad budget based on ROI
- Consider hiring a freelancer for overflow work
- Add recurring revenue services (SEO, maintenance)
- Explore partnerships with local business organisations

### Phase 4: Expansion (Year 2+)
- Hire first employee or regular freelancer
- Expand service offerings
- Target specific niches (e.g., restaurants, trades)
- Consider white-label services for other agencies
