## Video Production Tips

### Equipment Needed
- Smartphone (iPhone or decent Android)
- Ring light (£15-20 from Amazon)
- Tripod/phone holder (£10)
- Free editing: CapCut

### Video Structure (hook in first 1-3 seconds!)
1. **Hook** (0-3 sec): "This restaurant was losing customers because..."
2. **Problem** (3-8 sec): "They had no website and people couldn't find them online"
3. **Solution** (8-20 sec): "So I built them this..." [show the website]
4. **Result** (20-30 sec): "Now they get 50+ bookings every week"
5. **CTA** (last 3 sec): "DM me 'website' for a free quote"

### Hooks That Work
- "Stop scrolling if you own a small business"
- "This is what a £299 website looks like"
- "POV: you're a business owner who just got a new website"
- "I built this in 5 days"
- "This business was invisible online until..."
- "You're losing money if your website looks like this"
- "The secret to getting customers online"

## Growth Strategy

### Month 1: Foundation
- Post 5-7 videos per week
- Focus on before/after content (highest engagement)
- Engage with other creators in the small business niche
- Reply to every comment

### Month 2: Consistency
- Start using trending sounds with web design angles
- Create a series: "Website Wednesday" — weekly website reveals
- Collaborate with small business owners for content

### Month 3: Conversion
- Add strong CTAs to videos
- Create a "DM me WEBSITE" automation
- Use TikTok's business features (link in bio, etc.)
- Consider TikTok Ads if organic is working
