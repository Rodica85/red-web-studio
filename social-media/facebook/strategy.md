## Facebook Groups Strategy

### Groups to Join (UK business groups)
1. UK Small Business Network
2. UK Startups & Entrepreneurs
3. Small Business UK
4. Local town/city business groups (search "[your town] business network")
5. Women in Business UK
6. UK Freelancers & Self-Employed

### How to Engage in Groups
- **DON'T** spam your services
- **DO** answer questions about websites, tech, online presence
- **DO** share free tips and value
- **DO** help people with website issues
- **THEN** when someone asks "anyone know a web designer?" — you'll be top of mind

### Example Group Comments
- "Great question! For a small business, I'd recommend [helpful advice]. Happy to chat more if you need."
- "I work in web design and this is something I see a lot. Here's what I'd suggest..."

## Facebook Ads Strategy

### Phase 1: Awareness (Month 1-2, £5-10/day)
- **Audience:** Small business owners in UK, 25-55 age
- **Interest targeting:** Small business, entrepreneurship, Shopify, Wix
- **Ad type:** Video ad (before/after website transformation)
- **CTA:** "Learn More" → landing page

### Phase 2: Leads (Month 3+, £10-20/day)
- **Ad type:** Lead form ad
- **Offer:** "Free Website Audit" or "Free Consultation"
- **Retarget:** People who visited your website or engaged with posts

### Ad Copy Templates

**Ad 1 — Problem/Solution:**
> Still relying on word of mouth? In 2026, your customers search online first.
> At RED Web Studio, we build affordable, professional websites for small businesses across the UK.
> Starting from just £299.
> Book your free consultation today.

**Ad 2 — Social Proof:**
> "RED built our website in just 5 days and we've had 30+ new enquiries this month!" — Sarah K., Glow Beauty
> Ready for your turn? Get a free quote today.

**Ad 3 — Offer:**
> This month only: Get a professional business website for just £299 (normally £499).
> Mobile responsive. SEO included. Live in 5 days.
> Limited spots available. DM us or visit our website.
